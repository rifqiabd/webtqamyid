# Content Strategy

## Principles
- Content-first: typography is the primary design element
- Authentic, human, community-oriented, educational, faith-centered
- Tell a story about the institution's history, mission, and impact
- Indonesian language throughout
- No generic marketing language or corporate buzzwords

## Content Hierarchy per Page

### Home
- Hero: value proposition + CTA
- Introduction: welcoming message
- Core Values: 4 value cards
- Education Units: 3 unit cards
- Programs: 6 program cards
- Statistics: counters
- CTA: admission

### About
- History narrative
- Founder biography
- Vision & Mission
- Organizational structure

### Education Units
- Per-unit detail pages with curriculum, facilities, achievements

### Programs
- 6 program detail pages with curriculum, methods, outcomes

### News
- Content collection with tags, dates, categories
- Individual article pages

### Gallery
- Filtered grid (activities, facilities, events)
- Lightbox view

### PPDB
- Requirements, process, fees, download links, CTA

### Contact
- Address, phone, WhatsApp, email, social media, form, map
